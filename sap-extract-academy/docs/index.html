A practical field guide to extracting SAP S/4HANA data — without the licensing surprises, performance bottlenecks, or 3 AM incidents.

Every page covers the SAP-side reality first: released CDS views, authorization requirements, volume characteristics, and the gotchas that break pipelines. Tool-specific configuration comes second, wrapped in a picker so you see only what's relevant to your stack.
