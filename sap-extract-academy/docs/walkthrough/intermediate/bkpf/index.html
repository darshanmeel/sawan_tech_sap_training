Extract BKPF Multi-Year with Python ODP

Full extraction of multiple fiscal years (2022-2024), partitioned by BUKRS and GJAHR. Python/pyrfc with parallel loading.

Estimated time
75 minutes
Destinations
Snowflake
Extractors
Python

License check

All licenses.

Overview

Scenario

Your finance warehouse needs 3 years of accounting documents. You'll extract in parallel by fiscal year and company code to minimize extraction time.

What you've built

You've mastered multi-dimensional partitioning (fiscal year × company code) and parallel extraction. Finance can now run ad-hoc reports across years without waiting for sequential loads.

Before you start

  • Completed BKPF Beginner
  • Python/pyrfc expertise
0 of 4 steps complete
  1. Step 1

    Design multi-year extraction strategy

    Extract 2022, 2023, 2024 in parallel. Each year partitioned by BUKRS for further parallelism.

    Verify
    9 extraction tasks planned (3 years × 3 companies).
  2. Step 2

    Execute parallel extractions

    Use ThreadPoolExecutor to run up to 4 parallel pyrfc connections. Each extracts one (BUKRS, GJAHR) partition.

    Verify
    All 9 partitions complete in ~30 minutes (vs sequential 90+ minutes).
  3. Step 3

    Load into Snowflake with partitioned schema

    Store in ACCOUNTING.BKPF_RAW partitioned by GJAHR and BUKRS.

    Verify
    ACCOUNTING.BKPF_RAW has 9 partitions, all populated.
  4. Step 4

    Reconcile aggregate totals

    For each fiscal year and company code, sum amounts in Parquet and compare to SAP via FM_GET_DOCUMENT_HEADER RFC (if available).

    Verify
    Total amounts match within 0.01% (allowance for rounding).

Troubleshooting

ThreadPoolExecutor exhausts SAP connection pool

Limit to 4 parallel threads. Queue remaining tasks.
